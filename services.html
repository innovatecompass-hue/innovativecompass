Services

Four disciplines. One way of thinking.

Everything we do begins the same way: with a careful look at the business. Then, and only then, do we decide what to build.

01
Strategic Audit & Advisory

A clear look at how the business actually runs.

Problem it solves

You know something's off. You can't quite name it. Every quarter the same frictions return — and the same workarounds.

What changes

A documented map of how your business actually operates — where it leaks time, where it loses money, where it creates work that shouldn't exist. Paired with a prioritized sequence for fixing it.

Outcome

Clarity. Direction. A plan that makes sense of the last 12 months and sets the next.

02
System Design & Automation

Work that completes itself.

Problem it solves

Your team spends more time moving information between tools than using it. Manual work has quietly become the job.

What changes

We design the underlying flow of information in your business — what happens when a lead arrives, a client signs, a project closes — and build the automation that makes it move on its own.

Outcome

Fewer handoffs, fewer mistakes, a team that can finally think.

03
AI Visibility (GEO)

Show up where decisions are forming.

Problem it solves

Your customers have started asking AI the questions they used to Google. If you're not appearing in those answers, you're invisible to a growing share of your market.

What changes

We audit your presence across AI search surfaces — AI Overviews, ChatGPT, Perplexity — and rebuild the signals (content, llms.txt, crawler access, structured passages) that determine whether you get cited.

Outcome

You show up where decisions are forming. Not later — when they're already made.

04
Data & Reporting Infrastructure

One version of the truth. Quietly maintained.

Problem it solves

You have data in ten places and one version of the truth in none.

What changes

A single, quiet reporting layer that pulls from the tools you already use — and tells you what's actually happening in the business, updated on its own.

Outcome

You stop guessing. Meetings get shorter. Decisions get sharper.
